Who I Am

I'm a dual-degree engineering student pursuing a BE in Computer Science from BNMIT, Bengaluru, and a BS in Data Science from IIT Madras — driven by a deep fascination with the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity.

My work focuses on designing intelligent systems that aren't just performant, but secure. From Graph Neural Networks for social engineering detection to eBPF-based kernel-level monitoring, I build solutions that operate at the bleeding edge of what's possible.

I believe in building from first principles. Whether it's understanding how transformers process token embeddings or how eBPF probes intercept syscalls, I want to know the "why" as deeply as the "how." Low-level systems thinking is at the core of everything I do.

Currently open to research collaborations, internships, and opportunities where AI meets security at scale. If that resonates, let's talk.
